Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains exactly what data KAYA collects, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under the Ghana Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843).

1 Who We Are

KAYA ("we", "our", or "us") is a food and goods delivery platform connecting customers, vendors, and delivery riders in Ghana. The KAYA mobile application is operated by KAYA Technologies.

The data we collect depends on whether you use KAYA as a customer, vendor, or rider.

3 How We Use Your Data

  • Providing the service: Processing orders, matching riders to deliveries, calculating delivery routes, and enabling real-time tracking.
  • Payments: Facilitating customer payments via Paystack and disbursing earnings to vendors and riders via Mobile Money.
  • Account verification: Reviewing documents submitted by vendors and riders to ensure safety and trust on the platform.
  • Notifications: Sending order status updates, delivery alerts, and important account notices via push notification or email.
  • Safety and fraud prevention: Detecting and preventing unauthorized account access, fraudulent orders, or platform abuse.
  • Improving the platform: Understanding usage patterns to fix bugs, improve features, and build a better experience.
  • Legal compliance: Meeting our obligations under Ghanaian law and responding to lawful government requests.

5 Location Data

Customers: We use your saved home location to show nearby vendors and estimate delivery distance. We do not track your location in the background.

Riders: During an active delivery, your GPS location is shared in real-time with the assigned customer so they can track their order. Location sharing stops as soon as the delivery is marked complete. We do not track rider location outside of active deliveries.

6 Identity Documents

Vendors and riders are required to submit verification documents (e.g. Ghana Card, driver's license). These are stored securely in encrypted cloud storage and are only accessible to KAYA platform administrators for the purpose of verifying your account. Documents are not shared with customers or other third parties.

7 Data Retention

  • Active account: We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted account: When you request deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records (e.g. financial transaction records).
  • Order records: Order history may be retained for up to 7 years for accounting and dispute resolution purposes.

8 Data Security

We protect your data using industry-standard measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies so users can only access their own data
  • Authentication tokens with automatic expiry
  • Access controls limiting which staff can view sensitive data

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If a data breach occurs that affects your rights, we will notify you as required by the Ghana Data Protection Act.

9 Children's Privacy

KAYA is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has registered on our platform, please contact us immediately and we will delete the account.

10 Your Rights

Under the Ghana Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Object to how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at rsarpong6328@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
